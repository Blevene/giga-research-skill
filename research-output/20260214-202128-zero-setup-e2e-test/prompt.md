# Research Task: History of the Turing Award

## Scope
Brief overview of the ACM Turing Award — its origins, purpose, and a few notable recipients.

## Key Questions
1. When was the Turing Award established and by whom?
2. Who were the first three recipients and what were their contributions?
3. What is the current prize amount?

## Depth & Focus
Keep it concise — 1-2 paragraphs per question. This is a quick validation test.

## Output Expectations
Short report (under 1000 words) with inline citations where possible.
